Who I Actually Am

I'm Athul. From Alappuzha, Kerala. Did my BSc CS at Calicut University, now in my second year of MCA at KTU. It's going fine, thanks for asking.

Since then: Kaggle, courses, random datasets at weird hours. Six projects built, certifications done, more Jupyter time than I'd like to admit. No professional experience yet. All of it is real effort.

I want to be in a room where people care about the data. Internship, part-time, doesn't matter. Just give me something to actually work on.
